Project Management

Goyang Stadium, Korea

 

Led a team of draughtsmen in the detail re-design of the stadium roof under severe time constraints. The design provided the fabric with a double curvature the roof design comprised PTFE coated fibre glass fabric, tensioned over tied arched purlins that spanned between lattice tubular steelwork cantilevered trusses. The roof comprises of 22 individual panels of PTFE membrane, giving a total fabric area of 15100 square meters. This involved detail consideration of sophisticated and aesthetic connections such as the intersection between the steel truss and its supports. Work also involved the coordination between external membrane specialists, contractors and architects in Korea. Client: IIIA architects. Complete design of steel structure supporting the fabric roof, Coordinating between architect, engineers and fabrication, Checking Design of subcontractors’ membrane Calculations

 

Shams Tower 1, Plot 37, Abu Dhabi

 

Project management of an iconic 340m high tower on an island close by the centre of Abu Dhabi, which will be situated between further adjacent developments forming a visual gateway to a new city. The tower 1 is part of the plot 37 that consists of approximately 100,000m2 floor area for the podium, mainly serving for parking purposes, and approximately 200,000m2 floor area for the tower 1. – Project Management in the Concept and Scheme Design Phase, which included the Early Works Tender Package and commissioning an aeroelastic wind tunnel test. Client: RW Armstrong & primary client Sorouh, Architects: Arquitectonica in Hong Kong

 

Moonshow, Arcadia Cruise

 

Design of a 12m high x 23m wide arched performance structure located on the pool deck of the cruise ship Arcadia. This project involved the design of a welded and bolted aluminium structure, and considered dynamics and temporary structural stability.
